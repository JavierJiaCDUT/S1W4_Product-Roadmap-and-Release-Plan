## 学习目标 (Learning Objectives)

### 知识层面 (Knowledge)
**Knowledge Level:**

1. 理解产品路线图（Product Roadmap）和发布计划（Release Plan）的定义、目的和区别
   Understand the definitions, purposes, and differences between Product Roadmap and Release Plan

2. 了解敏捷估算的特点和故事点（Story Points）的概念
   Understand the characteristics of Agile estimation and the concept of Story Points

3. 认识敏捷规划与传统规划的核心差异
   Recognize the core differences between Agile planning and traditional planning

### 技能层面 (Skills)
**Skills Level:**

1. 能够制定基本的产品路线图，包含主题、时间框架和关键里程碑
   Be able to create a basic product roadmap, including themes, timeframes, and key milestones

2. 能够规划发布计划，确定发布范围、目标和依赖关系
   Be able to plan a release plan, determining release scope, goals, and dependencies

3. 能够使用扑克牌估算法（Planning Poker）进行故事点估算
   Be able to use Planning Poker for story point estimation

### 应用层面 (Application)
**Application Level:**

1. 能够将产品愿景转化为可执行的路线图和发布计划
   Be able to transform product vision into executable roadmaps and release plans

2. 能够应用速度（Velocity）概念预测团队交付能力
   Be able to apply the Velocity concept to predict team delivery capacity

3. 能够将敏捷规划实践与 PMBOK 知识领域关联
   Be able to map Agile planning practices to PMBOK knowledge areas

### 引入：从"愿景"到"行动"的桥梁 (5 分钟)
### Introduction: The Bridge from "Vision" to "Action" (5 minutes)

回顾与承上启下：
Review and Transition:
- "前几周，我们从创新想法出发，通过用户故事和用户故事地图，将模糊的需求细化并结构化，并确立了产品愿景。"
- "In the past few weeks, we started from innovative ideas and refined vague requirements through user stories and user story mapping, establishing a product vision."
- "现在，我们有了愿景和一堆排好优先级的需求，但如何把它们变成可执行的、有节奏的开发计划？这就需要敏捷项目规划。"
- "Now we have a vision and a prioritized set of requirements, but how do we turn them into an executable, rhythmic development plan? This requires Agile project planning."

敏捷项目规划理念：
Agile Project Planning Philosophy:
- 核心： 适应性、迭代性、渐进性。
- Core principles: Adaptability, iteration, and progressiveness.
- 区别于传统规划： 传统规划强调详细、固定，敏捷规划强调灵活、响应变化。
- Difference from traditional planning: Traditional planning emphasizes detailed and fixed plans, while Agile planning emphasizes flexibility and responding to change.
- 目标： 在不确定性中提供方向，并允许在学习中调整。
- Goal: Provide direction amid uncertainty and allow adjustments through learning.

### 产品路线图（Product Roadmap）的制定 (15 分钟)
### Creating a Product Roadmap (15 minutes)

定义与目的：
Definition and Purpose:
- "产品路线图是产品随时间演进的高层次视图，它沟通了产品未来的发展方向、关键里程碑和交付的价值。"
- "A product roadmap is a high-level view of how a product evolves over time, communicating the product's future direction, key milestones, and value to be delivered."
- "它不是一个甘特图式的详细计划，而是一个战略性文档，回答'我们未来要去哪里？'。"
- "It is not a Gantt chart-style detailed plan, but a strategic document that answers 'Where are we heading in the future?'."

产品路线图的构成要素：
Key Components of a Product Roadmap:
- 愿景 (Vision)： 路线图的根本驱动力。
- Vision: The fundamental driving force of the roadmap.
- 主题/目标 (Themes/Goals)： 驱动产品发展的关键业务目标或战略重点（例如：提升用户留存率、拓展新市场）。
- Themes/Goals: Key business objectives or strategic priorities driving product development (e.g., improving user retention, expanding into new markets).
- 时间框架 (Timeframes)： 通常是季度或月份，而非具体日期。
- Timeframes: Typically quarters or months, not specific dates.
- 关键成果/里程碑 (Key Outcomes/Milestones)： 每个时间段内期望达到的重要成果或交付物。
- Key Outcomes/Milestones: Important achievements or deliverables expected within each time period.
- 不包含： 具体的用户故事、任务细节。
- Does not include: Specific user stories or task details.

路线图的类型 (简要提及)：
Types of Roadmaps (Brief mention):
- 基于目标 (Goal-Oriented Roadmap)： 强调要解决的问题或达成的目标。
- Goal-Oriented Roadmap: Emphasizes problems to solve or goals to achieve.
- 基于产品 (Product-Oriented Roadmap)： 强调要发布的功能。
- Product-Oriented Roadmap: Emphasizes features to release.

制定方法：
Creation Methods:
- 从产品愿景出发。
- Start from the product vision.
- 结合用户故事地图中的"切片"（发布版本）。
- Combine with "slices" (release versions) from the user story map.
- 与干系人沟通，达成共识。
- Communicate with stakeholders to reach consensus.

路线图的特点： 战略性、高层次、灵活、持续更新。
Characteristics of Roadmaps: Strategic, high-level, flexible, continuously updated.

工具： 简单的表格、PPT、专门的路线图工具（如 Productboard, Aha!）。
Tools: Simple tables, PPT, dedicated roadmap tools (e.g., Productboard, Aha!).

### 发布计划 （Release Plan）的制定 (10 分钟)
### Creating a Release Plan (10 minutes)

定义与目的：
Definition and Purpose:
- "发布计划是产品路线图的下一层，它更具体地说明了在特定发布版本中要交付哪些功能，以及何时交付。"
- "A release plan is the next layer below the product roadmap; it more specifically describes what features will be delivered in a particular release and when."
- "它将高层次的路线图主题转化为可执行的功能集。"
- "It translates high-level roadmap themes into executable feature sets."

与路线图的区别： 路线图是战略性的"要去哪儿"，发布计划是战术性的"怎么去"。
Difference from Roadmap: The roadmap is strategic "where to go," while the release plan is tactical "how to get there."

发布计划的构成要素：
Key Components of a Release Plan:
- 发布目标 (Release Goal)： 本次发布的核心目的。
- Release Goal: The core purpose of this release.
- 发布范围 (Release Scope)： 本次发布包含的关键功能（来自产品待办事项列表）。
- Release Scope: Key features included in this release (from the Product Backlog).
- 发布日期 (Release Date)： 预期的交付时间。
- Release Date: Expected delivery time.
- 依赖关系 (Dependencies)： 内部或外部的依赖。
- Dependencies: Internal or external dependencies.
- 风险 (Risks)： 可能影响发布的风险。
- Risks: Risks that may impact the release.

制定方法：
Creation Methods:
- 从产品待办事项列表中选择高优先级、高价值的功能。
- Select high-priority, high-value features from the Product Backlog.
- 结合团队的容量（Velocity）进行规划。
- Plan based on the team's capacity (Velocity).
- 与开发团队紧密合作，共同承诺。
- Work closely with the development team to make joint commitments.

发布节奏： 强调敏捷推荐小步快跑、频繁发布，以便快速获取反馈。
Release Cadence: Emphasize Agile's recommendation for small, frequent releases to quickly gather feedback.

### 敏捷估算技术入门 ：故事点估算 (10 分钟)
### Introduction to Agile Estimation: Story Point Estimation (10 minutes)

为什么需要估算？
Why Do We Need Estimation?
- 帮助团队理解工作量。
- Help the team understand the workload.
- 帮助产品经理做发布计划。
- Help Product Owners make release plans.
- 但不作为个人绩效考核工具。
- But should not be used as a personal performance evaluation tool.

敏捷估算的特点：
Characteristics of Agile Estimation:
- 相对估算 (Relative Sizing)：不直接估算小时数，而是与其他故事比较。
- Relative Sizing: Do not directly estimate hours, but compare with other stories.
- 团队共同估算：增强团队对工作的理解和承诺。
- Team estimation: Enhances team understanding and commitment to the work.
- 不确定性：承认估算的不精确性，随着信息增多而细化。
- Uncertainty: Acknowledge the imprecision of estimates, refining them as more information becomes available.

故事点 (Story Points)：
Story Points:
- 定义： "一个抽象的度量单位，代表完成一个用户故事所需的工作量、复杂度和不确定性的综合体现。"
- Definition: "An abstract unit of measure that represents the combined effort, complexity, and uncertainty required to complete a user story."
- 优势： 避免直接估算时间带来的压力和不准确性，更关注相对大小。
- Advantages: Avoid the pressure and inaccuracy of directly estimating time, focusing more on relative size.

估算方法：扑克牌估算 (Planning Poker) (简要演示或讲解流程)：
Estimation Method: Planning Poker (Brief demonstration or explanation of the process):
- 团队成员共同参与，每人持一套斐波那契数列（1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21...）扑克牌。
- Team members participate together, each holding a set of Fibonacci sequence (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21...) cards.
- 讨论一个用户故事，然后同时出牌。
- Discuss a user story, then reveal cards simultaneously.
- 如果估算差异大，则进行讨论，直到达成共识或相对一致。
- If there are significant differences in estimates, discuss until reaching consensus or relative agreement.

速度 (Velocity)：
Velocity:
- "团队在一个迭代周期内完成的故事点总和。"
- "The sum of story points completed by the team within one iteration cycle."
- 用于预测未来迭代的交付能力，辅助发布计划。
- Used to predict future iteration delivery capacity and assist in release planning.
- 强调：速度是团队自己的度量，不应作为比较工具。
- Emphasis: Velocity is the team's own metric and should not be used as a comparison tool.

### 关联 PMBOK 知识领域 (5 分钟)
### Mapping to PMBOK Knowledge Areas (5 minutes)

规划绩效域 (Planning Performance Domain)：
Planning Performance Domain:
- 敏捷路线图和发布计划是敏捷方法下"规划"的具体体现。PMBOK 强调规划是一个渐进明细和迭代的过程，这与敏捷规划理念高度一致。
- Agile roadmaps and release plans are concrete manifestations of "planning" under Agile methods. PMBOK emphasizes that planning is a progressive elaboration and iterative process, which is highly consistent with Agile planning philosophy.
- 估算方法也是规划绩效域的重要组成部分。
- Estimation methods are also an important component of the Planning Performance Domain.

开发方法和生命周期绩效域 (Development Approach and Life Cycle Performance Domain)：
Development Approach and Life Cycle Performance Domain:
- "交付节奏"是该绩效域关注的重点。敏捷路线图和发布计划正是定义了产品的交付节奏和迭代周期。
- "Delivery cadence" is a key focus of this performance domain. Agile roadmaps and release plans precisely define the delivery cadence and iteration cycles of the product.
- 敏捷开发方法（如Scrum）的生命周期就是由一系列迭代（Sprint）和发布构成的。
- The life cycle of Agile development methods (such as Scrum) consists of a series of iterations (Sprints) and releases.

估算方法 (PMBOK Tools & Techniques)：
Estimation Methods (PMBOK Tools & Techniques):
- PMBOK 中有多种估算技术，敏捷中的故事点估算属于其中一种相对估算方法。
- PMBOK contains multiple estimation techniques; story point estimation in Agile is one type of relative estimation method.
- 强调估算的目的是为了更好地规划和管理预期，而非给出绝对精确的数字。
- Emphasize that the purpose of estimation is to better plan and manage expectations, not to provide absolutely precise numbers.

**教学策略与建议：**
**Teaching Strategies and Recommendations:**

1. 概念清晰： 敏捷规划与传统规划有显著区别，需要反复强调敏捷的适应性、迭代性特点。
1. Clear Concepts: Agile planning differs significantly from traditional planning; repeatedly emphasize Agile's adaptive and iterative characteristics.

2. 图示辅助： 使用图表清晰展示产品路线图和发布计划的层级关系，以及故事点估算的流程。
2. Visual Aids: Use diagrams to clearly show the hierarchical relationship between product roadmaps and release plans, as well as the story point estimation process.

3. 互动练习：
3. Interactive Exercises:
		- 可以给一个简单的产品愿景，让学生尝试勾勒出几个高层次的路线图主题。
		- Provide a simple product vision and have students try to outline several high-level roadmap themes.
		- 对于故事点估算，可以模拟一个简单的扑克牌估算环节，让学生体验一下。
		- For story point estimation, simulate a simple Planning Poker session to let students experience it.

4. 强调"柔性"： 敏捷规划是灵活的，路线图和发布计划都会随着学习和反馈而调整。
4. Emphasize "Flexibility": Agile planning is flexible; both roadmaps and release plans will be adjusted based on learning and feedback.

5. **课后思考/小作业：**
5. **Post-class Reflection/Mini-assignment:**
		- "请为你的'校园二手物品交易App'（或其他自选产品）制定一个简单的产品路线图，包含至少3个主题和时间框架。"
		- "Create a simple product roadmap for your 'Campus Second-hand Trading App' (or other product of your choice), including at least 3 themes and timeframes."
		- "选择其中一个主题，尝试规划一个MVP的发布计划，并列出其中的关键功能。"
		- "Select one theme and try to plan an MVP release plan, listing the key features."
		- "思考：故事点估算相比于小时估算，有哪些优势和劣势？"
		- "Reflect: What are the advantages and disadvantages of story point estimation compared to hour-based estimation?"
